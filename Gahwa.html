Emirati Arabic Coffee – A traditional taste of hospitality

Gahwa
30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of water
  • 1 cup of Finjan grinded coffee beans
  • 1 teaspoon of cardamom
  • Half teaspoon of clove
  • A pinch of saffron

Equipment

  • A Dallah (Arabic coffee pot)
  • Arabic coffee cups (Finjan)

Steps

  1. Start by roasting the coffee beans until fragrant.
  2. Allow the coffee beans to cool down.
  3. Grind the cooled coffee beans finely.
  4. Grind the cardamom separately.
  5. Bring 1 liter of water to a boil in a pot.
  6. Let it boil for about 10 minutes.
  7. Prepare the Dallah by pre-warming it.
  8. Add 1 teaspoon of ground cardamom into the Dallah.
  9. Add half a teaspoon of clove (optional).
  10. Pour the brewed coffee into the Dallah carefully.

Tips

  • For an authentic taste, use freshly roasted and ground beans.
  • Use a high-quality saffron to enhance the aroma.
  • Serve Gahwa with dates or traditional sweets for a true Emirati experience.
  • Adjust the amount of cardamom and clove according to your taste preference.
